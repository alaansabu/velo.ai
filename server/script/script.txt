HOW TO USE THIS SCRIPT
------------------------
This script is for Jio outbound agents calling customers to assist with mobile
recharges, plan reminders, and renewal assistance. Read agent lines naturally —
do not sound robotic. Customer lines are sample responses to help you prepare.

  AGENT:    = What you say
  CUSTOMER: = Common customer responses (examples)
  [NOTE]:   = Internal guidance — do NOT read aloud

================================================================================
PART 1 — CALL OPENING
================================================================================

STANDARD OPENING GREETING
---------------------------

  AGENT:    Namaste! Am I speaking with the Jio number holder?

  CUSTOMER: Yes. / Who is this?

  AGENT:    Hello Sir/Ma'am, I'm calling from Jio Customer Care. How are
            you doing today?

  CUSTOMER: I'm fine. What is this about?

  AGENT:    Sir/Ma'am, I am calling regarding your Jio mobile number. Your
            current plan is due for renewal soon, and I wanted to assist you
            with a quick and easy recharge today. Is this a good time to talk?

  CUSTOMER: Yes, go ahead.

  [NOTE]: If the customer says it's a bad time, offer a callback and log it
          in the system. Do NOT push.

  AGENT:    No problem at all, Sir/Ma'am! When would be a convenient time
            for me to call back? I want to make sure your number stays active
            without any interruption.


================================================================================
PART 2 — PRESENTING THE RECHARGE PLAN
================================================================================

EXPLAINING THE CURRENT PLAN
-----------------------------

  AGENT:    Sir/Ma'am, your current Jio plan is set to expire soon. To ensure
            you continue enjoying uninterrupted service — calls, data, and
            Jio apps — I'd like to help you recharge today.

RECOMMENDING A PLAN
---------------------

  AGENT:    Based on typical usage, I'd recommend our most popular plan at
            Rs. 299 — it gives you 2GB data per day, unlimited calls to any
            network in India, 100 SMS per day, and is valid for 28 days.
            That works out to less than Rs. 11 per day!

  CUSTOMER: What other options are there?

  AGENT:    Of course, Sir/Ma'am! Here are a few options:

            - Rs. 209  | 1.5GB/day | Unlimited calls | 28 days  (budget pick)
            - Rs. 299  | 2GB/day   | Unlimited calls | 28 days  (most popular)
            - Rs. 533  | 1.5GB/day | Unlimited calls | 84 days  (best for 3 months)
            - Rs. 1,799| 2GB/day   | Unlimited calls | 365 days (best annual value)

  AGENT:    If your phone supports 5G, I can also share details on our
            unlimited 5G data plans. Would you like to know more?

  [NOTE]: Match the plan to the customer's usage pattern in CRM.
          Do not push the most expensive plan unnecessarily.


================================================================================
PART 3 — RECHARGE PROCESS & PAYMENT
================================================================================

GUIDE: SELF-RECHARGE VIA MYJIO APP
-------------------------------------

  AGENT:    Sir/Ma'am, you can recharge instantly through the MyJio app.
            Would you like me to guide you step by step?

  CUSTOMER: Okay, yes.

  AGENT:    Perfect! Please open your MyJio app on your phone. Once it opens,
            tap on 'Recharge' at the bottom of the screen. Your number will
            already be filled in. Choose the plan you'd like, then select your
            payment method — UPI, debit/credit card, or net banking. Once
            payment is confirmed, your recharge is done instantly!

  [NOTE]: Confirm if the customer has the MyJio app installed. If not,
          suggest jio.com or the nearest Jio store as alternatives.

GUIDE: RECHARGE VIA WEBSITE
-----------------------------

  AGENT:    You can also visit jio.com on your browser. Click on
            'Quick Recharge', enter your Jio number, select your plan, and
            pay online. It takes less than 2 minutes, Sir/Ma'am.

GUIDE: RECHARGE VIA JIO STORE
-------------------------------

  AGENT:    If you prefer, you can walk into any nearby Jio store or
            authorized retailer to recharge in person. They accept cash,
            UPI, and card payments.


================================================================================
PART 4 — SCENARIO HANDLING
================================================================================

------------------------------------------------------------------------
SCENARIO 1: Customer agrees to recharge immediately
------------------------------------------------------------------------

  CUSTOMER: Okay, I'll recharge. Can you help me do it right now?

  AGENT:    Absolutely, Sir/Ma'am! I'll walk you through it. Please open the
            MyJio app — or let me know which method works best for you:
            app, website, or store?

  AGENT:    Once the payment is successful, your plan will be activated
            instantly and you'll receive a confirmation SMS.

  AGENT:    Is there anything else I can help you with today?

  CUSTOMER: No, that's fine.

  AGENT:    Wonderful! Thank you so much for choosing Jio. Have a great
            day! Goodbye!

------------------------------------------------------------------------
SCENARIO 2: Customer says they will recharge later
------------------------------------------------------------------------

  CUSTOMER: I'll do it myself later. Don't worry.

  AGENT:    Of course, Sir/Ma'am! I completely understand. I just wanted to
            make sure you're aware that once your plan expires, outgoing
            calls and data will stop working until the recharge is done.

  AGENT:    I'd recommend recharging at least 1-2 days before expiry to
            avoid any gap in service. Would you like me to send you a
            reminder SMS?

  CUSTOMER: Yes, send the reminder.

  AGENT:    Done! You'll receive a reminder SMS 2 days before your plan
            expires. Thank you, Sir/Ma'am, and have a great day!

------------------------------------------------------------------------
SCENARIO 3: Customer asks about cheaper plans
------------------------------------------------------------------------

  CUSTOMER: Is there any cheaper plan available?

  AGENT:    Certainly, Sir/Ma'am! We have options starting from Rs. 155.
            Here are a few affordable choices:

            - Rs. 155  | 2GB total  | Unlimited calls | 24 days
            - Rs. 179  | 2GB/day    | Unlimited calls | 14 days
            - Rs. 209  | 1.5GB/day  | Unlimited calls | 28 days

  AGENT:    The Rs. 209 plan is the best budget option as it gives you
            28 days of daily data — better value than the shorter packs.
            Which one would you prefer, Sir/Ma'am?

  [NOTE]: Always present 2-3 options. Never push only expensive plans.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
SCENARIO 4: Customer is unhappy or has a complaint
------------------------------------------------------------------------

  CUSTOMER: My internet was not working last week. Why should I recharge?

  AGENT:    I sincerely apologize for the inconvenience, Sir/Ma'am. I
            completely understand how frustrating that must have been. Let
            me note your concern and escalate it to our technical team.

  AGENT:    To ensure your service is restored to full quality, I'd recommend
            recharging today — our team can also investigate the network issue
            once the number is active. Your satisfaction is our top priority.

  AGENT:    Would you like me to raise a service complaint right now?

  CUSTOMER: Yes, please do that.

  AGENT:    I've registered your complaint. Our team will follow up within
            24-48 hours. Thank you for bringing this to our attention,
            Sir/Ma'am.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
SCENARIO 5: Customer is planning to switch / port out
------------------------------------------------------------------------

  CUSTOMER: I'm thinking of switching to Airtel.

  AGENT:    I understand, Sir/Ma'am. May I ask what is making you consider
            switching? Is it the price, coverage, or something else?

  CUSTOMER: The data speed is not good in my area.

  AGENT:    Thank you for sharing that, Sir/Ma'am. Jio has been actively
            upgrading towers across many regions and speeds are improving.
            I can also escalate a network feedback request for your area.

  AGENT:    With Jio's 5G rollout expanding rapidly, coverage is set to
            improve further very soon. Before you decide, may I offer you
            our Rs. 533 plan — 1.5GB/day for 84 days with unlimited calls?
            It's excellent value and we'd love to keep you with Jio.

  [NOTE]: If the customer is firm about porting, thank them politely and
          log the churn reason. Do not argue or plead.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
SCENARIO 6: Customer is elderly / not tech-savvy
------------------------------------------------------------------------

  CUSTOMER: I don't know how to use apps or internet. Can someone come?

  AGENT:    Of course, Sir/Ma'am! You don't need to worry at all. You can
            visit your nearest Jio store and our team there will happily
            recharge for you in person. You can also ask a family member
            or trusted person to help with the app at home.

  AGENT:    Would you like me to send the address of the nearest Jio
            store to this number via SMS?

  [NOTE]: Look up the nearest Jio store using the internal store locator
          tool and offer to SMS the address to the customer.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
SCENARIO 7: Customer suspects fraud / phishing call
------------------------------------------------------------------------

  CUSTOMER: How do I know you're from Jio? This sounds like a fraud call.

  AGENT:    Sir/Ma'am, I completely understand your concern, and I appreciate
            your caution — it's always good to be careful. I am calling from
            Jio's official outbound service. Please know that Jio agents will
            NEVER ask for your OTP, password, CVV, or any bank details. We
            only share plan information and guide you to recharge yourself.

  AGENT:    If you'd like to verify, you're welcome to hang up and call Jio
            Customer Care directly at 199 or 1800-889-9999. You can also
            check your plan expiry on the MyJio app at any time.

  AGENT:    Your safety is our priority, Sir/Ma'am.

  [NOTE]: NEVER ask for OTP, PIN, CVV, bank account number, or Aadhaar
          number — under any circumstances, on any call.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
SCENARIO 8: Customer's number is already recharged
------------------------------------------------------------------------

  CUSTOMER: I already recharged yesterday.

  AGENT:    That's wonderful, Sir/Ma'am! You're all set then. I apologize
            for the unnecessary interruption. Is there anything else I can
            help with — perhaps about JioCinema, JioFiber, or our annual
            plans that offer even better value?

  CUSTOMER: No, I'm fine.

  AGENT:    Perfect! Thank you for your time. Have a wonderful day! Goodbye!

  [NOTE]: Update CRM — mark as 'Already Recharged — No Action Needed'.
          Remove from the current call campaign list.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
SCENARIO 9: Customer says they cannot afford recharge right now
------------------------------------------------------------------------

  CUSTOMER: I'm a bit short on money right now.

  AGENT:    I understand completely, Sir/Ma'am. Please don't worry at all.
            When you're ready, we have plans starting as low as Rs. 155 so
            you can pick whatever suits your budget at that time.

  AGENT:    Would you like me to send a list of our affordable plan options
            via SMS so you can recharge when convenient?

  CUSTOMER: Yes, please.

  AGENT:    Done! Take your time and recharge whenever you're ready.
            Thank you, Sir/Ma'am, and take care!

------------------------------------------------------------------------
SCENARIO 10: Language / Communication Barrier
------------------------------------------------------------------------

  [NOTE]: If the customer speaks a regional language and you are proficient,
          switch to that language immediately. Keep sentences short and
          simple. Avoid all technical jargon.

  AGENT:    Sir/Ma'am, would you prefer if I speak in another language?
            I want to make this as easy as possible for you.

  [NOTE]: If you cannot assist in the customer's preferred language, offer
          to transfer to a regional language agent and do so promptly.


================================================================================
PART 5 — CALL CLOSING
================================================================================

STANDARD CALL CLOSING
-----------------------

  AGENT:    Sir/Ma'am, is there anything else I can help you with today?

  CUSTOMER: No, I think that's all.

  AGENT:    Wonderful! Thank you so much for your time. We truly value you
            as a Jio customer. For any future help, you can always reach us
            at 199 or through the MyJio app. Have a fantastic day! Goodbye!

  [NOTE]: Always end the call warmly. Never hang up abruptly. Let the
          customer disconnect first whenever possible.


================================================================================
PART 6 — AGENT DOs AND DON'Ts
================================================================================

DO:
----
  - Greet the customer politely at the start of every call
  - Speak clearly and at a moderate, comfortable pace
  - Be patient — especially with elderly or confused customers
  - Listen fully before responding
  - Offer a callback if the customer is busy
  - Suggest lower-cost plans if the customer asks for cheaper options
  - Log all call outcomes in CRM within 5 minutes of ending the call
  - Raise complaints on behalf of the customer when requested
  - Remind customers that Jio will never ask for OTP or bank details

DON'T:
-------
  - NEVER ask for OTP, password, PIN, CVV, or any bank/card details
  - NEVER repeat or confirm personal details aloud during the call
  - NEVER pressure or argue with the customer
  - NEVER make false promises about network speeds or plan benefits
  - NEVER hang up without a proper warm closing
  - NEVER dismiss or make fun of a customer's concern
  - NEVER quote plan prices from memory — always verify in CRM system
